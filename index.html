Sneha's best receipe for somosa

Ingredients

For the dough

  • Flour
  • Salt
  • Refine
  • Butter

For the Filling

  • Potato
  • Red chilli
  • Turmeric powder
  • Maggie masala
  • Onion
  • Green chili
  • Ginger
  • Garic

instructions

  1. Take one cup Flour add salt after that add Refine and butter and mix until combined into a dough
  2. Knead the dough for 15 mins
  3. Transfer the dough into a large bowl and wrap it with a plastic wrap and leave it for 2 hours
  4. Now let's make the filling for somosa
  5. Put the skillet on the gas and put some oil into it and leave it for a minute now put the onion,green chilli and garlic and ginger paste into the same and let it fry for a minute or two
  6. Now let's add potato, salt and turmeric powder into it and stir it well
  7. Now take the dough and add the filling into it and and wrap it into a triangle shape
  8. Now put the somosa into the oil and fry it until its brownish in colour
  9. And our somosa is ready to eat.
